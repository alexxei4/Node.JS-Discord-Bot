These slash commands use the X-colors API, and the functions are random colour ( no arguments) , random color via range (1  argument) , random colour via hue ( 1 argument),  random colour via hue and type (1 argument) , rgb to hex ( 3 arguments ) , and rgb to hsl ( 3 arguments). 

Also here are the commands i used to carry out these functions 

none - randomcolour
100 - randomcolourviarange
aqua - randomcolourviahue
pink , dark - randomcolourviahueandtype
100-100-100 - rgb to hex
100-100-100 - rgb to hsl
